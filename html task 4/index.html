ABOUT

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, as he explores the Grand Line in search of the mythical treasure known as the "One Piece" to become the King of the Pirates.

SEASONS
SEASON 1 (WATER 7 ARC)
EPISODE 1

The Straw Hat Pirates arrive at Water 7 and face CP9.


EPISODE 2

EPISODE 3

SEASON 2 (WANO)

EPISODE 2

EPISODE 3

The final battle begins against Kaido and Orochi’s forces.


SEASON 3 (FUTURE ISLAND ARC)
